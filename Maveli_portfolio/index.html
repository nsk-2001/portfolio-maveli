About Me



Heyy there!!, I'm Mahabali from pathalam.I was running my kingdom like any good ruler, step by step. But guess what? Along came Vamana, a crafty fella with a knack for changing heights. You won't believe it—I offered my own noggin as the final step, thinking I was outsmarting him. Talk about a royal blunder, right?

Now, every Onam, I pop up like an unexpected guest. It's all in good fun, a time to share laughs, yummy food, and remember that sometimes, even kings can be "stepping stones"!
